About Quanty

Quanty is an open-source, AI-driven market research tool designed to run locally on your machine. We built Quanty to bridge the gap between complex market analysis and intuitive research experiences.

Our Mission

We believe that powerful financial insights should be accessible and free. By leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs) and efficient CLI tools, Quanty empowers individual investors and researchers to analyze market trends without relying on expensive Terminal platforms.

Why CLI First?

Graphical interfaces are great, but for enthusiasts and power users, the Command Line Interface provides unmatched speed and composability. Quanty is built for those who love control and customization.
